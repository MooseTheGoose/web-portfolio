A Story Illustrating Multi-Tenacy

A Word of Warning

This story does not get to the point. This is a fun, fantastical piece which I aim to make entertaining more than informative, though I hope this one way of illustrating multi-tenancy is insightful.

The Story

Once upon a time, there was a fair and kind lady named Alice. She was born in the kingdom of Disperus. It was a great kingdom with amazing agriculture and mechanical industry, but it didn't value technology as much, especially the internet. There was still a lot of controversy about it.

Alice's family were people who were okay with it, so they had a computer and a router in their house. They didn't let Alice touch the computer until she was old enough. When she was 16, they saw how amazing and wonderful of a lady she turned out to be and decided to trust her with computer and internet access.

Alice absolutely loved the computer and the internet. She ended up spending much of her free time learning about software, ranging from operating systems to web services. She would go on internet forums under the user name of xXKingzOfSpadezXx and actively engage in conversations online. She especially adored the land of Crofox, which was responsible for much of the internet and computer manufacturing.
